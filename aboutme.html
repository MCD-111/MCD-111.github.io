About Me

Malcolm Caruana Dingli is a multi-instrumentalist who has achieved the Associate Diploma in drums from Trinity College London and Grade 8 in electric guitar. He is currently under the tuition of Argentinian drummer Mariano Steimberg. Caruana Dingli has achieved an Advanced Diploma in Performing Arts from the Malta College of Arts, Sciences & Technology and is currently in his second year of Bachelor Degree studies in the same field.

Supported by Arts Council Malta, Caruana Dingli has participated in Berklee College of Music’s Summer Performance Program in Valencia in 2021 and 2022, as well as the Berklee at Umbria Jazz Clinics in Perugia in 2023. He attended an Erasmus+ traineeship in Zaragoza with LocoCorp, a start-up company that specialises in STEAM knowledge through robotics, where he composed music for their video library.

Caruana Dingli has performed in a variety of public concerts, both locally and abroad, including a performance at the closing ceremony of the 2022 Special Olympics Invitational Games, which was broadcast live on national television. He is a committed drummer of a local heavy metal tribute band, Iron Knight, and a guitarist in the acoustic duo Reflections. He is also employed as a part-time audio producer with popular radio station 89.7 Bay.
